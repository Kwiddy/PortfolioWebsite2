Please use a browser that supports "canvas"

Camera Movement:

  • Arrow Keys: use the arrow keys to rotate the camera around an origin
  • WASD Keys: use the WASD keys to move the camera around the scene
  • Z/X Keys: use the Z and X Keys to zoom in and out of the scene
Animations:
  • C: Use the C key to set the table, this pulls out four chairs and builds four plate objects
  • P: Use the P key to move the pouffe to the other side of the sofa
  • T: Use the T key to turn on the TV
Lighting Controls:
  • L: Increase General Scene Lighting to a maximum point (initial light settings are maximum)
  • K: Decreases General Scene Lighting to a minimum point (zero light)
  • M: Increase Lamp Light
  • N: Decrease Lamp Light
Objects (x15 Total, inc. 11 Animated):
  • 1x Table
  • 4x Chairs
  • 1x Sofa
  • 1x Lamp
  • 1x Pouffe
  • 1x TV
  • 1x TV Stand
  • 1x TV Remote
  • 4x Plates & Cutlery
